I served as the product owner, the user interface designer, the user experience co-designer, and as a member of the engineering team with a focus on the front end.

Super Road Trip Advisor

For my team's "legacy project" at MakerSquare, we chose to build upon our classmates' fabulous original Road Trip Advisor. We added features, improved output, refactored, and refreshed the user interface.

I was responsible for re-writing algorithms which sorted and selected attractions, improving the quality and distribution of results. I also worked on front end improvements.
